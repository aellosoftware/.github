# Support

Start with the repository README, documentation, release notes, and existing issues.

## Product and technical support

- Use a repository issue for a reproducible bug or public technical question.
- Use the repository's feature-request template for product suggestions.
- Email support@aellosoftware.com when the matter should not be discussed publicly or no suitable repository exists.

Include the product or repository name, version, operating environment, expected behavior, actual behavior, and minimal reproduction steps. Remove credentials, private URLs, and personal data.

Aello is independently operated and does not promise a response time unless a specific product publishes a service commitment.

## Security and privacy

Do not use public issues for vulnerabilities, credentials, customer data, or other sensitive material. Follow SECURITY.md or email security@aellosoftware.com. Privacy questions may be sent to privacy@aellosoftware.com.
